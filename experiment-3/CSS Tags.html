Career Journey

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as “Captain Cool”, is one of the most successful captains in cricket history. He made his international debut in 2004 and quickly became known for his calm leadership, finishing skills, and wicketkeeping excellence.

Captaincy

Dhoni led India to victory in all major ICC tournaments — T20 World Cup 2007, 50-over World Cup 2011, and the Champions Trophy 2013.

World Records

  • Most wins by an Indian captain across all formats.
  • First captain to win all three ICC trophies.
  • Over 10,000 runs in ODIs with an average above 50.

Fun Facts

Dhoni worked as a Train Ticket Examiner (TTE) before becoming a cricket star.

He is married to Sakshi Dhoni and has a daughter named Ziva.

Born: July 7, 1981, Ranchi, India

Nicknames: Mahi, Thala, Captain Cool

IPL Team: Chennai Super Kings (CSK)
